What to do with Organic Waste?

Organic waste includes Papers,food scraps, yard waste, and other biodegradable materials. To manage organic waste responsibly:

    Compost kitchen scraps like fruit and vegetable peels.

    Use a compost bin for yard waste, leaves, and grass clippings.

    Avoid putting organic waste in regular trash to reduce landfill waste.

What to do with Recyclable Waste?

Recyclable waste includes items like plastics, glass, paper, and metal. To recycle effectively:

    Separate recyclables from regular trash.

    Check local recycling guidelines for specific materials that can be recycled.

    Reduce waste by choosing products with minimal packaging.
