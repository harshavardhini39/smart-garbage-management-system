Welcome to Waste Classifier, an interactive tool designed to help you sort your waste efficiently.

How it Works

Simply upload an image of waste, and our classifier will determine whether it's organic or recyclable.

Why Sort Waste?

Proper waste sorting is crucial for environmental sustainability. It ensures that materials are recycled or disposed of correctly, reducing the impact on our planet.

What to Do with Organic Waste?

Organic waste, such as food scraps and yard waste, can be composted. Composting turns this waste into nutrient-rich soil for plants.

What to Do with Recyclable Waste?

Recyclable waste, including paper, plastic, and glass, can be recycled. Recycling reduces the need for raw materials and minimizes pollution.
